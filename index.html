A Sip of Nature's Best

Paper Boat is a brand of traditional Indian beverages and foods produced and marketed by Hector Beverages, which is headquartered in Bengaluru, India.It was launched by Hector Beverages in August 2014. It comprises traditional Indian drinks such as Aam Panna, Jaljeera and Aam Ras. The drinks were initially offered in single serving, flexible pouches, the company has since then expanded to one liter Tetra Pak cartons. View All Products
